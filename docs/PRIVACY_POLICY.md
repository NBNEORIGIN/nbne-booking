## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Retention Periods

**Booking Data:**
- Active bookings: Duration of service + 2 years
- Cancelled bookings: 2 years from cancellation
- Completed bookings: 2 years from completion

**User Accounts:**
- Active accounts: Indefinite (while active)
- Inactive accounts: 1 year after last login
- Deleted accounts: Anonymized immediately

**Audit Logs:**
- Authentication logs: 1 year
- Data access logs: 2 years
- Security events: 2 years
- General activity: 90 days

**Financial Records:**
- 7 years (legal requirement)

### 5.2 Deletion

After the retention period, data is either:
- Permanently deleted, or
- Anonymized (personal identifiers removed)

## 6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You can request a copy of your personal data.

**How to exercise:**
- Email: privacy@nbnesigns.co.uk
- Subject: "Data Subject Access Request"
- We will respond within 1 month

### 6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can request correction of inaccurate data.

**How to exercise:**
- Update your account information directly, or
- Email: privacy@nbnesigns.co.uk

### 6.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.

**How to exercise:**
- Email: privacy@nbnesigns.co.uk
- Subject: "Right to Erasure Request"
- We will assess and respond within 1 month

**Limitations:**
- We may retain data if legally required
- Audit logs retained for legal compliance
- Anonymized data may be retained

### 6.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can request we limit how we use your data.

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

### 6.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 6.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.
